# Seppmail-HealthChecks

Synedat-led health checks, operational readiness reviews and evidence-oriented verification patterns for SEPPmail-related environments.

## Why this repository exists

Use this repository as a customer-facing technical starter for discovery workshops, pilot preparation, post-change validation and recurring operational reviews.

## What you will find here

- architecture and implementation notes
- security, compliance and control-mapping content
- example scripts and reusable patterns
- operations, troubleshooting and observability guidance
- demo scenarios, landing-page copy and workshop material
- source notes for product claims, partner context and image origin

## Who this is for

- customers evaluating operational baselines for SEPPmail-related environments
- architects and engineers preparing pilots or handovers
- operations teams who need reviewable checks and evidence bundles
- Synedat delivery teams using a public, reusable technical conversation asset

## Positioning

SEPPmail publicly presents the Secure Email Gateway with capabilities such as automatic encryption and decryption, support for common encryption standards, GINA, monitoring, reporting and integration into existing IT environments. This repository turns that product context into a practical operations and delivery conversation layer. See docs/SEPPMAIL-REFERENCES.md for the official product reference.
